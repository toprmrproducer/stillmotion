Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your files never leave your device

StillMotion processes every photo and video entirely inside your browser tab using WebAssembly (ffmpeg.wasm and libheif-js). Your image is never uploaded to any server, never transmitted over the network, and never stored by us in any form. When you close or refresh the tab, everything is discarded.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic (e.g. page views, referrers). This does not include the content of any file you convert.

Advertising

StillMotion may display ads served by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense) to keep the tool free. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. You can control ad personalization through your browser or through Google's own ad settings.

Cookies

StillMotion itself does not set cookies to track you. Third-party ad or analytics providers embedded on this site may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the contact details listed on our About page.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
