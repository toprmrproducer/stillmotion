How it works

1

Choose a photo

Select any JPG, PNG, WebP, or an iPhone HEIC photo — decoded right in your browser.

2

Pick duration & format

1 to 30 seconds, exported as MP4 (H.264) or WebM (VP9).

3

Download the clip

ffmpeg.wasm renders the video locally, then you download it directly. Nothing ever touches a server.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
